About Global Youth Forum

At MUN, GYF, participants immerse themselves in a realm where intellect meets action. Representing diverse countries, they collaborate to create solutions for global issues across 5 dynamic committees dedicated to diplomacy and problem-solving.

With over 300 delegates from 100+ colleges and schools in India, MUNC provides a platform for diverse perspectives and impactful dialogue. Expert Executive Boards offer invaluable guidance for an enriching experience.

Participants learn from inspirational speakers and experienced MUN veterans to gain valuable insights and motivation for success in diplomacy and problem-solving. Don't miss out on the chance to win exciting cash prizes and rewards at this engaging event!

Driving MUN excellence through comprehensive resources and connections.

About Bharat Vikas Parishad

The BVP, founded in 1963 on the birth anniversary of Swami Vivekananda is a right-wing voluntary organisation. It was initially formed with name - Citizens Council to mobilize people against Chinese attacks on Indian territory but was later renamed and registered as a society on 10 July 1963.

About Funology

Funology is a skill education-based organization that aims to transform traditional learning methods into dynamic, interactive, and entertaining experiences. We believe that when learning is enjoyable, individuals are more motivated, engaged, and likely to retain knowledge. Through a combination of cutting-edge technology, gamification, and experiential learning, Funology offers a diverse range of educational programs designed to unlock the full potential of learners.
